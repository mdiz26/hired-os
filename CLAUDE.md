## Project Writing Rules

- **Never use em dashes (Unicode U+2014, the long dash).** This applies to all writing: generated documents, evaluation reports, CVs, cover letters, application answers, tracker notes, and any user-facing copy. Use commas, periods, or parentheses instead. (`generate-pdf.mjs` already strips em dashes for ATS, but do not generate them in the first place.)
